CHECK DEPOSITS

Signing into the Bank's Mobile App: Customers log in to their bank's mobile app and select the account where they want to deposit the check. They also enter the check amount. Endorsing the Check: Customers need to sign the back of the check as they would for an in-person deposit. Some banks may require additional information like "For Mobile Deposit Only" on the back of the check for security purposes. Photographing the Check: Customers take photos of the front and back of the endorsed check using their mobile device's camera and upload these images to the app.
